NA Medicine

Dr. Nadeem Ashraf

Consultant Cardiologist · NHS, UK

A pioneer in interventional cardiology, Dr. Ashraf has performed over 4,000 life-saving procedures and trained the next generation of heart specialists.

SK Law

Saira Khan, KC

King's Counsel · Barrister, London

One of the first Pakistani-heritage barristers to be awarded King's Counsel, Saira is a trailblazer in human rights and immigration law.

RI Business

Raza Iqbal

Founder & CEO · TechBridge Group

Built a technology company from a Glasgow garage to a £40M enterprise employing 300 people, with offices across Europe and the Gulf.
